## Problem Section

### Headline
**Caymanian talent deserves better.**

### Problem 1: Caymanian Talent Is Invisible
Caymanians send resumes into the void. Employers use recruiters because there's nowhere else to look. Talent and opportunity never meet unless a recruiter connects them—and they take a 15-25% cut to do it.

### Problem 2: Recruiter Fees Are Crushing
15-25% of first-year salary per hire. That's CI$10-16K per person. A firm hiring 10 people a year wastes CI$90-150K in recruiter fees. That money could go toward growth. Toward salaries. Toward the island.

### Problem 3: Compliance Is A Mystery
Does the employer actually try to hire Caymanians? How do you prove it? Timestamped records, structured feedback, proof of effort—nobody's tracking it. The Caymanian preference system exists on paper. Not in practice.
