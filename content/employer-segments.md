## Ranking Methodology

**Score = Hiring Volume × Budget × Pain Level × Conversion Likelihood**

- Hiring Volume: 1-5 (annual hires)
- Budget: 1-5 (ability to pay)
- Pain Level: 1-5 (recruitment frustration)
- Conversion Likelihood: 1-5 (willingness to adopt platform)
