# careers.ky — Mission Statement

## Mission

**A Caymanian-First Careers Platform, making hiring talent visible, accessible and fair.**

## Vision

A Cayman Islands where every qualified Caymanian is genuinely considered for every role — and where employers hire better, faster, and without middlemen.

## Values

- **Caymanian-First:** Every decision starts with "does this serve Caymanian talent?" If it doesn't, we don't build it.
- **Fairness:** Every Caymanian deserves a fair shot, regardless of who they know. Merit, not networks.
- **Accessibility:** Free for job seekers, always. No barriers to being seen.
- **Transparency:** Both sides see the process. No hidden decisions. Earned over time, not promised upfront.
- **Community:** We succeed when Caymanians thrive. Profit follows impact.
- **Partnership:** We work with WORC, employers, and talent. We're infrastructure they all need — not a replacement for anyone.

## The Hierarchy

1. **Caymanians come first.** Without them, there is no platform.
2. **Employers come second.** We save them time and money — but only because Caymanian talent is on the platform.
3. **The system comes third.** We make the Caymanian preference system work as intended.

## What We Are Not

- We are not a recruiter. We replace recruiters.
- We are not a compliance tool. Compliance is a feature, not our identity.
- We are not anti-WORC. We complement WORC.
- We are not just another job board. We are infrastructure for a fairer labour market.
