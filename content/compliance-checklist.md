# Employer Compliance Checklist
### Are You Meeting All 6 WORC Requirements?

*Updated for the November 2025 rule changes. Print this page and use it for every hire.*

---

## Before You Post the Job

- [ ] **Job title** is accurate and industry-standard
- [ ] **Duties** are specific and detailed (not vague or generic)
- [ ] **Qualifications** list all required education, certifications, and experience
- [ ] **Salary** is stated as an exact figure or range (not "competitive" or "DOE")
- [ ] **Benefits** are fully itemized (health insurance, pension, housing, travel, etc.)
- [ ] **Working hours** are specified (full-time/part-time, weekly hours, shifts)
- [ ] **Job type** is stated (permanent, temporary, contract, seasonal)
- [ ] **Location** specifies the exact work location in the Cayman Islands
- [ ] **Special conditions** are noted (travel, physical demands, etc.)

> **How careers.ky helps:** Our job posting form requires every mandatory field. You physically cannot publish a non-compliant listing.

---

## During the Advertising Period (14+ Days)

- [ ] Job is posted on the **WORC Portal**
- [ ] Posting has been live for **14+ consecutive calendar days** (no gaps)
- [ ] You have **screenshot or timestamp proof** of posting dates
- [ ] All information in the posting **matches** what you'll submit in the permit application
- [ ] Posting has **not been edited** in ways that change core requirements mid-cycle

> **How careers.ky helps:** The platform syncs with the WORC Portal and timestamps every posting automatically. Compliance Reports include dated proof.

---

## Candidate Evaluation

- [ ] You have a **list of every Caymanian who applied**
- [ ] Each Caymanian applicant has a **documented outcome** (hired, interviewed, screened out, withdrew)
- [ ] Qualified Caymanian applicants were **interviewed**
- [ ] Interview dates, interviewers, and notes are **recorded**
- [ ] You searched for qualified Caymanians **proactively** (not just waited for applications)

> **How careers.ky helps:** Every Caymanian applicant is tracked with timestamps. Our Talent Search lets you proactively identify qualified Caymanians and document your outreach.

---

## Structured Feedback (For Every Non-Hired Caymanian)

- [ ] Each non-hired Caymanian has **written structured feedback**
- [ ] Feedback references **specific requirements from the job posting**
- [ ] Feedback explains **which qualifications, skills, or experience were lacking**
- [ ] Feedback is **2–5 sentences minimum** per candidate
- [ ] Feedback does **NOT** use generic language ("not a fit," "other candidates stronger")
- [ ] Feedback was created **during the hiring process** (not retroactively)

> **How careers.ky helps:** Compliance Reports generate structured feedback templates linked to your job requirements. Fill in specifics and the documentation is automatically formatted for Immigration.

---

## Work Permit Application Package

- [ ] **Proof of WORC advertising** with dates (14+ consecutive days)
- [ ] **Complete job posting** as it appeared on the portal
- [ ] **Full list of Caymanian applicants** with application dates
- [ ] **Outcome documentation** for every Caymanian applicant
- [ ] **Structured feedback** for every non-hired Caymanian
- [ ] **Interview evidence** (dates, notes, interviewers)
- [ ] **Comparative assessment** — why the work permit candidate was selected
- [ ] **CI$500 application fee** budgeted and ready
- [ ] All documentation is **contemporaneous** (created during the process)

> **How careers.ky helps:** One-click Compliance Reports compile everything above into a single PDF, formatted for Immigration submission and independently verifiable.

---

## Quick Self-Test: Will Your Application Survive Scrutiny?

Answer honestly:

1. Could an Immigration officer verify every claim in your application against WORC Portal records? **Y / N**
2. If a Caymanian applicant challenged your decision, does your feedback hold up? **Y / N**
3. Is every document dated and created during the hiring process (not after)? **Y / N**
4. Did you actively search for Caymanian candidates, or just wait? **Y / N**
5. Can you prove the 14-day advertising period was uninterrupted? **Y / N**

**If you answered "No" to any question, your application is at risk.**
