## FAQ Section

### Q: Is it really free?
**A:** Yes. Create your profile, get matched, get introduced. Zero cost to you. Employers pay us. You never will.

### Q: How long does it take to set up my profile?
**A:** About 10 minutes. Education, experience, skills, preferences. Done.

### Q: How many jobs are on careers.ky?
**A:** 933+ jobs from WORC, synced and updated weekly. Plus new jobs added directly by employers every day.

### Q: What if an employer sees my profile but doesn't introduce themselves?
**A:** careers.ky gives employers tools to find you. If they see you and you're not a match for their open role, they won't introduce themselves. That's fine—it means you're competing on merit, not on relationships.

But if you ARE a match, you'll hear from them. Or you'll see the role in your match alerts and introduce yourself.

### Q: Does careers.ky share my data with third parties?
**A:** No. Your profile is yours. You control what you share. We don't sell your data. We don't pass your information around.

WORC data is public (if you're already registered there). Everything else is private.

### Q: What if I want to update my profile later?
**A:** You can update your education, experience, skills, and preferences anytime. Changes take effect immediately.

### Q: Do I need a specific qualification to join?
**A:** No. If you're Caymanian or work in Cayman, you can join. If you're looking for work, you belong on careers.ky.

### Q: How do match alerts work?
**A:** You set your preferences (skills, location, job type). When a new job matches your profile, we email you and notify you in your dashboard. You see it first.

### Q: Can I see who viewed my profile?
**A:** Yes. You can see which employers have viewed your profile and sent you introductions.
