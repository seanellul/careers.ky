## 4. SEO Rationale: The .ky Domain Authority Loop

### The Strategy
Two .ky domains — one covering Cayman law (legislation.ky), one covering employer compliance and hiring (careers.ky) — create a **topical authority cluster** around Cayman Islands employment regulation.

### Why This Works

**Geographic relevance:** The .ky ccTLD is a strong geo signal. Google associates .ky domains with Cayman Islands content. Two authoritative .ky sites linking to each other reinforces both as the definitive sources for Cayman employment law and compliance.

**Topical complementarity:** legislation.ky provides the legal text; careers.ky provides the practical application. A user reading the Immigration Act on legislation.ky naturally wants to know "how do I comply?" — and careers.ky answers that. A user reading the compliance guide on careers.ky naturally wants to read the actual law — and legislation.ky provides it. These links serve genuine user intent.

**Link equity mechanics:** Each contextual, in-content link passes PageRank. legislation.ky Act pages linking to careers.ky guides boost careers.ky's authority for compliance queries. careers.ky pages linking to legislation.ky boost its authority for law-text queries. The loop amplifies both.

**Content freshness loop:** When legislation.ky indexes a new Act or amendment, careers.ky publishes practical guidance linking to it. When careers.ky publishes new compliance content, legislation.ky's "Practical Resources" sections link to it. Both sites stay fresh.

**Competitive moat:** No other .ky domain pair covers both the law text AND the practical employer compliance space. This topical lock creates a barrier to entry for competitors in Cayman employment search results.

### What This Is NOT
- Not a private blog network (PBN) — both sites have genuine, unique content serving different user needs
- Not reciprocal link manipulation — the links serve real user intent (law text ↔ practical guidance)
- Not link farming — links are contextual, in-content, and editorially placed

### Expected Impact
- **careers.ky:** Improved rankings for "WORC compliance," "Cayman work permit," and related employer queries
- **legislation.ky:** Improved rankings for "Cayman Islands [Act name]" and legal text queries
- **Both:** Increased domain authority within the .ky TLD space, making both sites harder to displace
