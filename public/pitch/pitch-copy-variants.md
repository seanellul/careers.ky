## A/B Test Plan

### Test 1: Subject Lines
- **Control:** Variant 1 (personalized with job count)
- **Challenger:** Variant 3 (risk/cost focused)
- **Metric:** Open rate
- **Sample:** 200 employers per variant

### Test 2: Deck Headline
- **Control:** Variant A (data-driven)
- **Challenger:** Variant C (risk-focused)
- **Metric:** Time on deck (scroll depth), CTA click rate

### Test 3: CTA
- **Control:** "Start Your Free Trial"
- **Challenger:** "See Your Compliance Report"
- **Metric:** Click-through rate
- **Note:** Test per segment — hospitality may prefer "See Report", legal may prefer "Schedule Demo"

### Test 4: Pricing Emphasis
- **Control:** Show both tiers equally
- **Challenger:** Pre-select recommended tier based on segment
- **Metric:** Conversion rate, average revenue per signup
