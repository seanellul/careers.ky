# Pitch Messaging Guide — careers.ky

## Who We Are

**careers.ky** is compliance automation for Cayman Islands employers. We solve the #1 problem created by the November 2025 WORC changes: documenting why Caymanians weren't hired, generating structured feedback, and producing one-click Compliance Reports for work permit applications.

We're not a recruiter. We're not a job board. We're the system that sits between your hiring process and WORC — making sure every permit application is bulletproof.

## Why careers.ky?

- **Automates compliance** — structured feedback collection, timestamped audit trails, one-click reports
- **Saves time** — 4-6 hours per permit application → 30 minutes
- **Saves money** — $299-799/mo vs. $15K-25K per denied permit or 15-25% recruiter fees
- **Reduces risk** — no more missed applicants, inconsistent documentation, or denied permits

## Why Now?

- **November 2025:** WORC changed the rules. New structured feedback requirements went live.
- **Confusion is high:** Most employers don't have a system for this. They're using email + spreadsheets.
- **Enforcement is starting:** Permits are being denied for insufficient documentation.
- **3,169 employers** are already in the WORC system — all affected, few prepared.
